SafePulse AI

Smart Personal Safety & Emergency Response Platform

A web-based safety platform that connects users with their trusted guardians through real-time monitoring and instant automated alerts.

02 / 07

Problem Statement

  • ⚠️ During emergencies, people may not be able to call or text for help.
  • 🔕 Traditional SOS systems wait for the user to press a button — which may be impossible.
  • ⏳ Every second of delayed response increases danger.
💡 SafePulse AI transforms emergency response from reactive to intelligent — detecting danger automatically, without any manual intervention.
03 / 07

Product Overview

Two connected interfaces working together in real time.

👤 User Portal
Simple one-time registration
Safety monitoring starts automatically
One-tap SOS button always accessible
Live sensor data transmitted to backend
🛡️ Guardian Portal
View live location on interactive map
Real-time risk score & safety status
Emergency signal alerts instantly shown
SMS notification sent when score hits 70
04 / 07

Key Features

🆘

SOS Trigger

One tap instantly sends location and a critical-level alert to the guardian.

📳

Shake Detection

Accelerometer detects violent shaking above 5 m/s² indicating struggle or panic.

❤️

Heartbeat Monitor

Camera-based rPPG tracks heart rate. Elevated BPM signals distress or panic.

🎤

Voice Monitoring

Microphone detects screams above 80 dB and contributes 35 points to the risk score.

📍

Live Location

GPS coordinates sent to guardian every 5 seconds with a Google Maps link in the SMS.

Innovation & What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional apps that only provide a button — SafePulse AI thinks on your behalf.

🧠

Intelligent Multi-Sensor Risk Score

Combines shake, sound, heart rate, and SOS into one unified risk score (0–100).

🔗

Real-Time Guardian Dashboard

Live updates via WebSocket every 5 seconds — no manual refresh needed.

⚡

Automatic SMS Trigger
